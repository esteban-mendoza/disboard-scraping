**Welcome to the best Role-Playing experience for Red Dead Online on Xbox!**

*Why is it the best?*

1. This community was founded because we felt that none of the existing roleplay servers fulfilled our desires and offered what’s best in roleplay - the freedom to interact and the ability to create your own path in the world of Red Dead Online.

2. What sets us apart from other servers is the fact that we have created a completely unique system that encourages players to roleplay in the game world, rather than forcing them to write everything on Discord. Other servers reminded us of creative writing groups - and we wanted to avoid that.

3. We learned from the best! This server is inspired by the best RP server on PC - WildRP. In fact, this server was created because its creators fell in love with a series on YouTube called "The Ballad of Bazz Kerrigan". If you know this story, you already know where you have just arrived.

4. We offer a unique system of connecting in the game - to play using our rules you don't have to wait for someone to organize an RP session - just log in to the game!

5. We accept only adults to our server. This makes the community full of mature people who want immersion and amazing interactions.

6. Thanks to the "interviews" system, we always know who becomes a member of our community, we know that everyone understands the rules and that we will be surrounded by responsible people when playing.

7. The system of economics, jobs, currency and other mechanics introduced by us is the best developed system that has existed so far. All the actions you can take encourage you to roleplay in-game, not on Discord.

8. Finally, we are a very friendly, open and relaxed group where you will be able to find new friends, relationships and more.
